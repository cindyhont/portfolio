<p>Built by me from scratch and hosted on multiple servers, with:</p>
<ul>
    <li><div class="modal-pm-tool-ul-title">Front end:</div>Next.js, Redux Toolkit</li>
    <li><div class="modal-pm-tool-ul-title">Back end:</div>Golang, Websocket, RabbitMQ</li>
    <li><div class="modal-pm-tool-ul-title">Database:</div>PostgreSQL</li>
    <li><div class="modal-pm-tool-ul-title">Server:</div>Nginx</li>
</ul>
<p>
A project management tool for corporate use, inspired by Jira and Asana. 
Every task can have unlimited levels of subtask, so that the project managers of big projects can go deeply into details.
Currently it offers list view and board view (kanban).
</p>
